Why you should tour Madagascar with MadaGuide?

We are travel experts in Madagascar

Everyone at MadaGuide lives in Madagascar and have extensive knowledge of the country. We have a range of itineraries to choose from and we can also plan bespoke tours to cover all of your needs. We can advise you on all things including transport, accomodation and experiences.

We have extensive experience touring in Madagascar

Our guides have years of experience touring in all regions of Madagascar. All guides speak fluent Malagasy, French and English. Our guides are very experienced driving through Madagascar's tricky terrain - you will be in safe hands with us at MadaGuide.

Explore this incredibly unique country

Madagascar is a melting pot of diverse cultures and traditions which is home to an amazing variety of wildlife and beautiful scenery. Join us to explore the iconic Baobab trees, spot leemurs in the wild, relax on stunning tropical beaches and see majestic humpback whales.
