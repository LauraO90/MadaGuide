MadaGuide Itineraries

The following itineraries are our most popular and can be combined for longer trips. If you have something different in mind, we can arrange bespoke trips to ensure you have the perfect experience.

Itinerary 1: Western Madagascar

Explore the iconic baobab trees by visiting the Avenue of the Baobabs and search for lemurs on this adventure.

  • Day 1: arrival in Antananarivo and check in to hotel.
  • Day 2: guided tour of the highlights of Antananarivo including local markets, Andafiavaratra Palace and the Royal Hill of Ambohimanga.
  • Day 3: long travel day from Antananarivo to Morondova, you will stop for lunch en route then see the Avenue of the Baobabs at sunset.
  • Day 4: free time in Morondova followed by a night visit to Kirinidy Forest to search for lemurs and nocturnal creatures .
  • Day 5: daytime visit to Kirindy Forest to search for lemurs and reptiles.
  • Day 6: daytime visit to explore the limestone formations in Tsingy de Bemaraha National Park.
  • Day 7: long travel day from Morondova to Antananarivo, you will stop to see the Avenue of the Baobabs during daylight and return to Antananarivo to catch your flight or continue your visit.

Itinerary 2: Northern Madagascar

Explore Nosy Be and its stunning surrounding islands for the perfect mix of snorkelling and relaxation.

  • Day 1: arrival in Antananarivo and check in to hotel.
  • Day 2: domestic flight to Nosy Be, your guide will meet you at the airport. Free afternoon for relaxation.
  • Day 3: day trip on speedboat to Nosy Komba (lemur island) to see lemurs and chameleons. The trip continues to Nosy Tanikely for an afternoon of snorkelling with tropical fish and several types of turtles. Lunch included.
  • Day 4: tour of southern parts of the island including the markets in the city of Hell-Ville and Cascade De Nosy Be waterfall.
  • Day 5: day trip on speedboat to the unspoilt beaches of Nosy Iranja for swimming and snorkelling. Lunch included.
  • Day 6: day trip on speedboat to Nosy Sakatia for snorkelling, look out for the green turtles. Lunch included.
  • Day 7: domestic flight to Antananarivo to catch your next flight or continue your visit.

Itinerary 3: Antananarivo and Eastern Madagascar

Explore the bustling capital city Antananarivo then explore the wildlife and biodiversity of the eastern rainforests.

  • Day 1: arrival in Antananarivo and check in to hotel.
  • Day 2: guided tour of the highlights of Antananarivo including Andafiavaratra Palace and the Royal Hill of Ambohimanga.
  • Day 3: free morning in Antananarivo. Afternoon drive from Antananarivo to Andasibe.
  • Day 4: day tour of Analamazaotra National park to see a variety of lemurs, reptile and birds.
  • Day 5: day tour of Vakona Lemur Island to visit lemurs who have been saved from illegal trafficking and poaching.
  • Day 6: travel from Andasibe to Antananarivo. Afternoon tour of the markets in Antananarivo.
  • Day 7: airport drop off to catch your flight or continue your visit.
